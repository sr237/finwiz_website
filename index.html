Who We Are

FinWiz is the official Finance & Innovation Club of NIT Warangal, dedicated to fostering financial literacy, investment acumen, and entrepreneurial spirit among students. We bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry practice through curated talks, workshops, and collaborative projects.

Our community comprises passionate students, alumni mentors, and industry professionals committed to building the next generation of financial innovators.

Collaboration

Impact
